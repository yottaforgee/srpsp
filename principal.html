Principal's Message

Shaping Minds, Building Futures

Dr. Priya Sharma

Principal, Sunrise Public School

Ph.D. in Education, 20+ Years of Experience

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to Sunrise Public School, where we are committed to providing an education that goes beyond textbooks and examinations. As the Principal of this esteemed institution, I am proud to lead a team of dedicated educators who share a common vision of nurturing young minds to become responsible global citizens.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world." - Nelson Mandela

At Sunrise Public School, we believe in creating a learning environment that:

  • Fosters intellectual curiosity and critical thinking
  • Encourages creativity and innovation
  • Promotes values of integrity, respect, and responsibility
  • Develops leadership and teamwork skills

Academic Excellence

Our curriculum is designed to challenge students while providing the support they need to excel academically.

Character Building

We emphasize the development of strong moral values and ethical principles in our students.

Global Perspective

Our students are prepared to face global challenges with confidence and competence.

Our teaching methodology combines traditional values with modern educational practices. We focus on:

  • Interactive and experiential learning
  • Technology integration in education
  • Regular assessment and feedback
  • Individual attention to each student

I invite you to join us in this journey of academic excellence and personal growth. Together, let's create a future where our students are not just successful professionals but also responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Warm regards,
Dr. Priya Sharma
Principal, Sunrise Public School
