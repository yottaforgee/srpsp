Our Academic Programs

At Sunrise Public School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to foster academic excellence and holistic development.

Primary Education

Our primary education program focuses on foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking.

Secondary Education

Our secondary education program prepares students for higher education and career readiness with a strong emphasis on STEM subjects.

Extracurricular Activities

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities to support students' interests and talents, including sports, arts, and clubs.

Advanced Placement Courses

Our AP courses provide students with the opportunity to earn college credit and prepare for higher education challenges.

Special Education

We offer tailored programs to support students with special needs, ensuring an inclusive and supportive learning environment.
